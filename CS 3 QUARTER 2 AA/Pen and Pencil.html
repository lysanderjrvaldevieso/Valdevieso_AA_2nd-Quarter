Pen and Pencil

Pens and pencils are an investment in the cornerstone of academic achievement, not just a formality to include on the back-to-school buying list. With their ability to record ideas, encourage creativity, improve memory, ease communication, and impart a feeling of accountability, these little instruments become indispensable allies in the learning process. The modest pen and pencil serve as more than just writing tools for pupils getting ready for a new school year; they are also vehicles for education, creativity, and personal development. The adage "the pen is mightier than the sword" is accurate when it comes to education.

Here are some pro's and con's for using pen and pencil:

Pro's:

  • Small and Convenient: Pens and pencils are small and thin which makes it ideal for pencil cases or pockets when needed on the go.
  • Longevity: Pens and pencils are long lasting such as pens having long lasting ink and pencils having lead.
  • Price and Accessibility: They can be found in any store and is also very cheap.

Con's:

  • Unsafe for toddlers and children: Pens and pencils have sharp and thin points to them and if left uncheck toddlers and children could hurt themselves with it.
