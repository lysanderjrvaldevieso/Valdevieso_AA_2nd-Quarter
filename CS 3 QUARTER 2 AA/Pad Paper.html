Pad paper

Pad paper should not be disregarded when kids go on their back-to-school purchasing binges. Its value as a fundamental learning tool, a note-taking tool, an organizer, and a focus and attention enhancer cannot be emphasized. Purchasing premium pad paper is an investment in kids' academic achievement as it gives them a concrete foundation to direct their educational path. Pad paper becomes increasingly important as the school year goes on, and every student should have it in their toolbox.

Here are some pro's and con's for using pad paper:

Pro's:

  • Different sizes: Pad paper come in a variety of large or small sizes and is perfect for any occasion.
  • Multi-purpose: Pad paper have many uses such as writing down notes, answering quizzes, and writing down your grocery list.
  • Price and Accessibility: Pad paper can be found in any store and is relatively cheap.

Con's:

  • Not waterproof: Pad paper isn't waterproof and that means that you have to be careful handling with it as this can damage it.
