Highlighter

Highlighters are a strategic investment in improving learning outcomes, not just a matter of personal taste when it comes to the back-to-school shopping list. Highlighters stand out as crucial tools for academic performance because of their capacity to concentrate attention, enable active reading, stimulate good note-taking, and support active study practices. The bright strokes of a highlighter become more than simply colorful marks on paper as children start a new school year; they become lights that lead the path to understanding, productivity, and structure. In the pursuit of knowledge and academic success, the modest highlighter—which is sometimes disregarded—proves to be an enlightening force.

Here are some pro's and con's for using highlighters:

Pro's:

  • Easily refillable: Refills are easily accessible and cheap to find in stores.
  • Different type and shapes: Different types of tips and sizes can easily be found in any store.
  • Many uses: Aside from highlighting your notes, it can also serve as a calligraphy tool or even can be used for coloring.

Con's:

  • Text damage: Some highlighters can actually bleed through the paper and onto the back and this could affect the text.
